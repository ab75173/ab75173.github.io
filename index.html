About

Adam is a DC-based Creative Technologist. He enjoys finding creative, outside the box solutions for web applications. He is currently enrolled in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program.

Before beginning his quest to take over the world, Adam was the Technology Project Associate at the Center for Shared Services where he created web applications and managed the help desk internally, as well as for client organizaitons. He has extensive non-profit and start-up experience.

After discovering he wanted to devote his full attention to web applications, Adam was determined to expanded his substantial skillset. When Adam is not in front of his 'battle station' he can be found running on the National Mall, hiking with his dog, or cooking bacon.
